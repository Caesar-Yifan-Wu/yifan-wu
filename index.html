I’m Yifan (Caesar) Wu, a first-year PhD Student in Innovation and Information Management and a member of the AI Evaluation Lab at HKU Business School.
My research primarily focuses on behavioral information systems, exploring fundamental features of human judgment, behavior, and decision-making—particularly in the context of interactions with emerging AI technologies. I am open to diverse methodologies that can strengthen my research. At the AI Evaluation Lab, I actively contribute to AI benchmarking and evaluation projects.
